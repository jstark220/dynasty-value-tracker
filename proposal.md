# What am I Building and Who is it for? 
I am building an app for players of dynasty fantasy football to track the vallue of their players over time. Dynasty fantasy football is for particularly hardcore fans that keep and manage their same fantasy teams year-over-year. This website will store the value of their players and track them like stocks. 

# Why? What problem does it solve? 
I am a big dynasty player myself. I am in part building this tool for me because there is no good way to visualize how the value of my team has changed year-to-year, as well as showing the immediate relative strength of my team compared to the rest of the league. I know there are others like me that would benefit from this tool, so I am building it to fill that gap.

# MVP vs Stretch-Goals
The MVP would be a tool where individuals can upload individual players to the page and track the value over time. I would like to expand this though, by using Sleeper's API to incorporate league and team data tracking without the user needing to enter all the names of the player's individually. Beyond that, I could see this tool being applied to more fantasy sports other than football, and maybe even for general managers of real professional teams. 

# Technology I plan to Use
I will be using all of the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript we have learned as the tool will be very interacative, and visualization elements will be key. I will also use both the free FantasyCalc and Sleeper APIs for the player and league data for the tool.
